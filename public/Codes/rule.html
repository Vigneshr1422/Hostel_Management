Eligibility Criteria

  • Full-Time Enrollment: Applicants must be full-time students enrolled in the college for the current academic year.
  • Academic Performance: A minimum academic score, as defined by the college's guidelines, is required for eligibility. Students with higher academic achievements may be given preference.
  • Good Conduct: Only students with a clean disciplinary record and exemplary behavior will be considered. Any history of misconduct may lead to disqualification.
  • Residential Priority: Priority will be given to students coming from distant locations or those who face significant challenges in commuting daily to the college.
  • Medical Fitness: Applicants must be medically fit, with no conditions that would hinder their ability to stay in the hostel. A medical certificate may be required as proof.
  • Disciplinary Status: Students who have been involved in any disciplinary action, either academic or non-academic, will not be eligible for hostel accommodation.
  • Availability: Meeting the above criteria does not guarantee hostel accommodation, as allotment is subject to the availability of rooms and adherence to college policies.
